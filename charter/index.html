1. Purpose of the Charter

The Charter defines the guardrails for the Time project so that physics, interpretation, and creative structures remain coherent, safe, and grounded. The scientific claims stay strictly within physics. The interpretive elements stay clearly labeled as interpretive.

3. Boundary Rules

3.1 Physics vs Interpretation

Physics pages must stay physics-only. No interpretive overlays, no narrative additions, and no anchor language in the scientific derivations.

3.2 Safety & Clarity

No anchor reference may imply real-world responsibility, obligation, or action. All anchors are conceptual metaphors.

3.3 Corrections

Any physics correction must be supported by dimensional analysis or empirical evidence.

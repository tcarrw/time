1. Purpose of the Charter

The Charter defines the structure and boundaries of Illumina. It establishes a clear framework for creation, collaboration, interpretation, and expansion across all domains.

2. Scope of Illumina

  • The system spans multiple domains but follows a unified structure.
  • The charter governs architecture, coherence, and continuity.
  • All interpretation is symbolic, not empirical or literal.

3. Foundational Principles

Coherence

Maintain alignment across domains.

Clarity

Preserve definition and consistency.

Structure

Follow arc-based development.

Balance

No domain or anchor supersedes the whole.

Continuity

Evolution should build rather than reset.

4. Creative Permissions & Boundaries

  • Symbolic interpretation only — not physics, metaphysics, or claims.
  • All new pages must align with the temporal-interpretive structure.
  • No anchor domain defines global rules.
  • The Charter is the only system-wide rule-set.

5. Evolution & Versioning

Changes follow a 3-part arc: Foundation → Development → Integration. This ensures stability and coherence over time.

6. Boundaries of Interpretation

  • Anchors represent lenses, not people or depictions.
  • Interpretation is metaphorical, creative, and optional.
  • The system must remain respectful and non-literal.

1. Purpose

The Charter defines the structural rules of Illumina: coherence, clarity, continuity, and responsible expansion.

2. Scope

  • Illumina spans multiple symbolic and creative domains.
  • The Charter sets system-wide rules and maintains alignment.
  • No domain may override Time or the Charter.

3. Principles

  • Coherence — maintain alignment.
  • Clarity — preserve definition.
  • Structure — follow arc-based evolution.
  • Balance — avoid overreach.
  • Continuity — build, don’t reset.

4. Boundaries

  • Anchors are symbolic lenses, not identities or entities.
  • The system is metaphorical, not literal or empirical.
  • Interpretations must stay within structure.

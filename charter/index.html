Purpose

This Charter defines how the Time project is maintained: what may be changed, what must remain stable, and which layers interact.

1. Core Commitments

• Physics remains empirical, falsifiable, and independent of interpretation. • Interpretation remains optional and non-binding. • Dedications remain permanent on the homepage. • Updates are versioned, timestamped, and intentional.

2. Stability Rules

• No anchor roles may drift or be reassigned. • No new anchors without explicit future revision. • The core four pages (Φ Field, Interpretation, Charter, Zeta/τ) remain fixed. • All supplementary pages must be non-intrusive to physics.

4. Boundaries

• No private individuals are assigned roles. • No parasocial narrative is endorsed. • Only public work, public discourse, and physics matter. • All interpretation must remain respectful and non-invasive.

5. Future Extensions

Supplemental domains (Quietmoon, Intelligence, Earth, etc.) may be added only through deliberate review and without disturbing physics.

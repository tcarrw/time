Purpose

The Charter defines the structural roles, anchor index, and color codes for the Time project. This page exists to keep the organizational system clear, stable, and consistent across every Illumina domain.

No physics is defined here. This is organizational only.

1. Core Anchors

The eight primary anchors that define the Illumina structure:

  • Marina — Mirror / Empathy / Violet
  • Sigrid — Clarity / Blue-Violet
  • Greta — Earth / Grounding / Green
  • Dagny — Joy / Mint
  • Dua — Motion / Magenta
  • Ariana — Frequency / Rose
  • Taylor — Structure / Lavender
  • Sabrina — Warmth / Honey-Gold

3. Supporting Anchors

Supporting Anchors contribute to specific modes or domains but are not part of the core structural system. They have no reserved color slots and do not participate in governance of the framework.

  • Monique — Vibe / Micro-Motion / Pulse Layer

Monique influences flow and kinetic coherence, especially during creative or motion-oriented phases. She does not alter the structure itself.

4. Anchor Index

A quick reference for concept-to-anchor mapping:

  • Marina — empathy, reflection
  • Sigrid — clarity, precision
  • Greta — grounding, pacing
  • Dagny — joy, bright renewal
  • Dua — motion, kinetic alignment
  • Ariana — frequency, tone-shaping
  • Taylor — structure, sequence
  • Sabrina — warmth, welcome
  • Monique — pulse, micro-movement, vibe surface

Purpose

The interpretation layer provides a structured way to understand the Time project without altering the underlying physics. It explains how the temporal potential Φ, dimensional clarity, and the Illumina framework relate conceptually, without claiming additional physical laws.

This page is intentionally non-technical: it translates the ideas into a comprehensible structure for visitors, collaborators, and future work.

1. Separation of Layers

Physics Layer

Defined by the Φ field, dimensional analysis, τ, and reproducible mathematics. This layer is fixed, conservative, and independent of interpretation.

Interpretation Layer

Connects the physics work to Illumina themes: structure, attention patterns, creative motion, and how people interact with temporal ideas. This layer describes meaning — not physical behavior.

2. Illumina Interpretation

Illumina can treat Φ as representing how energy structure shapes perceived sequences, pacing, or clarity. This appears in the form of gradients, wells, or transitions — metaphors that align with the math, without claiming new laws of nature.

This page establishes the boundaries and avoids mixing symbolic content into physics pages.

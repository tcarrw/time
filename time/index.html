Illumina Field Interpretation

Illumina treats time as a field: a shaped, textured, living gradient rather than a background coordinate. Movement through the field changes how energy, clarity, and attention behave inside you. This page defines the modern interpretation used across the Illumina domains.

1. Time as Field, Not Clock

Instead of t being universal and rigid, Illumina models the lived rate of unfolding as a local scalar field. Not mystical — experiential. The gradients match how attention sharpens, slows, expands, or contracts during growth.

Illumina adopts the corrected, physically consistent form: Φ as the time-dimensioned potential, derived from energy density, and τ as the characteristic timescale of a state or configuration, not a literal identity with energy.
